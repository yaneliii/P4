Inspirations

Costa Daniel Kassab

Fig. 1. Bluey background color roughs from Kassab, Costa Daniel. “Bluey.” Costa Daniel Art, www.costadanielart.com/
concept-art#/bluey.

Costa Daniel Kassab is the lead art director for Bluey; the following pictures are some of his concept art and color roughs from the show. His masterful use of color-blocking captivated me as it infused the show with life and warmth that made you feel calm. Color is a powerful tool; I admire how Kassab not only applies it for practical readability but also to drive emotional depth. His approach ensures that each color scheme and direction maintains the show's signature fun and brightness. Additionally, the soft rounded lines used in the world of Bluey contribute to its enchanting charm, offering a balance between realism and whimsy. This 'shape language' crafts a world of simplicity, transporting viewers into a realm of joy and wonder.
